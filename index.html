Game Development

HamQuest

A hack & slash game where you play as a little hamster called Hammy who's on a quest to save a kingdom. Fight off hoards of evil cats who have taken over the kingdom.
This was my capstone project for Atlantic University. I worked on this video game as a programmer and artist, by developing several mechanics such as: movement, combat, and game progression. I also worked on the characters and level design.

A short story game where you have to investigate a murder in a small town.
